## Style Prompt

CareBridge AI is presented like a calm Apple product film made for real families: bright mineral-white space, precise typography, softly rounded iPhone frames, and one humane green accent. The story moves from fragmented care information toward a single shared rhythm. Technology stays quiet and legible; the emotional payoff is confidence during every handoff.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F5F7F4`
- Paper: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink: `#10251A`
- Care green: `#168653`
- Deep green: `#075E3A`
- Mist green: `#DDF2E5`
- Muted text: `#617368`
- Hairline: `#C9D8CE`
- Warning: `#E8AE32`
- Alert: `#D94A43`

## Typography

- Primary: `"Noto Sans JP"` for reliable embedded CJK rendering, with system sans fallbacks.
- Technical labels and timestamps: `"SF Mono"` with monospace fallback.
- Headlines use 700–800 weight and tight tracking; supporting text uses 350–450 weight.

## Motion

- One transition language throughout: 0.42-second blur crossfade with a subtle forward scale.
- Content is distributed across 19 concise scenes so each page carries one clear idea and moves with the upbeat music.
- Display copy enters character by character with compact staggered timing; supporting UI follows in a second beat.
- Major entrances land on the music’s strong attacks; secondary details overlap within 0.45 seconds.
- Every scene has one restrained ambient motion: drift, focus pull, device pan, status pulse, or timeline progress.
- A persistent bottom progress rail advances across the entire film.

## What NOT to Do

- No dark-tech gradients, neon, particle clouds, or generic AI imagery.
- No hard cuts between scenes.
- No static slideshow pages.
- No tiny interface text presented as the main message.
- No fear-based medical imagery or alarmist red flashes.
