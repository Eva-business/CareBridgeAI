## Style Prompt

CareBridge AI lives between a warm family care journal and a precise Apple-native health tool. The film uses a bright, calm canvas with tactile paper grain, generous white space, rounded device frames, and confident green structure. Human care always leads; technology appears as quiet, trustworthy assistance rather than spectacle.

## Concept Angle

A fragmented handoff becomes a shared bridge: scattered messages, languages, and memories gradually align into one clear care timeline.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F7FAF6`
- Paper: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink: `#183326`
- Primary green: `#1F8F57`
- Soft green: `#E0F5E8`
- Deep green: `#0D6840`
- Warning yellow: `#F2B833`
- Danger red: `#E64038`
- Muted text: `#557064`
- Hairline: `#C8DCCE`

## Typography

- Headlines and Traditional Chinese body: `"Noto Sans JP"` at 700 for headlines and 400 for body.
- Technical labels, timestamps, and framework names: `"Space Mono"` at 400 or 700.
- Headline tracking: `-0.04em`; body line height: `1.55`.

## Shape and Depth

- Device frames and major cards: 44–64px corners.
- Labels and chips: fully rounded pills.
- Shadows: soft green-tinted depth, never hard black shadows.
- Borders: 2–4px on a light canvas so structure survives video compression.

## Motion

- Overall energy: warm, assured, medium-low.
- Primary transition: blur crossfade, 0.65–0.8s.
- Accent transition: vertical push for major section changes.
- UI enters with precise slides and scale settles; ambient elements drift or breathe slowly.

## What NOT to Do

- No neon, cyan-on-black, or purple technology gradients.
- No generic identical-card grids.
- No tiny web-sized typography.
- No medical fear imagery or alarmist motion.
- No gratuitous AI particle effects; show the product and the care workflow.
